## ⚠️ CRITICAL DECISIONS REQUIRED

### Decision 1: Automated Pre-Screening

**Question:** Implement automated pre-screening (40% load reduction)?

**Option A: YES (Recommended)**
  Benefit: 40% load reduction, catches 85-90% of confusions, quality improves
  Cost: 80-120 hours engineering, 4-6 hours/month maintenance
  Timeline: 2-3 weeks to implement, 1 week testing
  Risk: Automation flaws could propagate (mitigated by peer review)
  
  VERDICT: STRONGLY RECOMMENDED
  Do not launch aggressive scenario without pre-screening.

**Option B: NO (Manual only)**
  Benefit: No automation risk, full manual control
  Cost: Reviewer burnout by month 6-9, quality degradation
  Timeline: Immediate (no implementation time)
  Risk: System cannot scale, cascading failures likely
  
  VERDICT: NOT RECOMMENDED
  Only viable for conservative scenario with strong team.

**Recommendation: IMPLEMENT PRE-SCREENING**

---

### Decision 2: Governance Board Formation

**Question:** Charter formal governance board (8 members) before launch?

**Option A: YES (Formal board before launch)**
  Benefit: Clear authority, quorum established, smooth operation
  Cost: Onboarding time (4-6 weeks), calendar scheduling
  Timeline: Start immediately, ready by month 2-3
  Risk: Members may not be available (mitigate with alternates)
  
  VERDICT: RECOMMENDED for moderate/aggressive scenarios
  
**Option B: PHASED (Start informal, formalize month 3)**
  Benefit: Faster initial launch, informal decisions okay initially
  Cost: Potential inconsistency early on
  Timeline: Formal board established by month 3-4
  Risk: First 3 months lack clear authority, decision conflicts
  
  VERDICT: ACCEPTABLE only for conservative scenario
  
**Option C: NO (No formal board yet)**
  Benefit: Minimal overhead, faster decisions
  Cost: Decisions inconsistent, lack of governance
  Timeline: Board never formalized (bad long-term)
  Risk: System breaks under growth
  
  VERDICT: NOT ACCEPTABLE
  
**Recommendation: CHARTER FORMAL BOARD (8 members) by month 2**

  **DO NOT LAUNCH AGGRESSIVE WITHOUT 3+ REVIEWERS & CHIEF SEMANTICIST**

### Decision 4: Fatigue Monitoring

**Question:** Implement automated fatigue monitoring (dashboard + alerts)?

**Option A: YES (Automated monitoring)**
  Benefit: Catches burnout early, prevents cascading failures
  Cost: 40-60 hours engineering, integrated into review platform
  Timeline: 2-3 weeks to implement
  Risk: If monitoring disabled, risks emerge quickly
  
  VERDICT: MANDATORY
  
**Option B: NO (Manual tracking only)**
  Benefit: No system overhead
  Cost: Burnout detected late, team collapse risk
  Timeline: Reactive (problem emerges, dealt with after)
  Risk: High cascading failure probability

**Recommendation: MANDATORY AUTOMATED MONITORING**
